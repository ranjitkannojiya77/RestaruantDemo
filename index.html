Our Story

Bella Vita (also known as Bella Vita Organic) was founded in 2012 by Anju Anand, Aashima Anand, and Aakash Anand. The company's origins are in a family-run salon where they developed homemade organic skincare and beauty products, which eventually grew into an online D2C (direct-to-consumer) brand.
